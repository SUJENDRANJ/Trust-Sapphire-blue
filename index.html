ABOUT US

Empowering Marginalized Communities in Tamil Nadu

Sapphire Blue Social Trust (SBST) is a registered NGO focused on uplifting women, differently-abled individuals, and children through sustainable education, health services, and skill development programs.

We collaborate with government bodies and educational institutions to deliver impactful training, healthcare camps, and livelihood initiatives that empower thousands across Tamil Nadu.

  • Registered under Indian Trust Act and FCRA approved for foreign contributions.
  • Dedicated to enhancing lives with sustainable, community-driven development.
