Medical Camp & Stationery Distribution

A free medical camp conducted to provide healthcare services to over 310 beneficiaries from SC communities along with distribution of stationery kits to support education.

Organized in partnership with Nantha Siddha Medical College, this initiative helped improve health awareness and educational access for underserved children.

Program Highlights:

  • Free health checkups & medicines
  • Distribution of stationery kits to children
  • Health awareness sessions
  • Special focus on SC communities
  • Collaboration with medical college
  • Impacted 310+ beneficiaries
  • Improved access to basic healthcare
  • Encouraged continued education

Handicraft Expo & Entrepreneurship Training

A platform to showcase women-led SHG products with 45 stalls exhibiting 250+ handicraft products, coupled with entrepreneurship training to promote self-reliance.

This project helped artisans gain market access and enhance business skills to support economic upliftment and gender equality.

Program Highlights:

  • Exhibition of 250+ handicraft products
  • Entrepreneurship training workshops
  • Women empowerment through market access
  • Skill development in embroidery, weaving
  • Collaboration with local artisans
  • 45 SHG stalls with diverse products
  • Business mentoring & market linkage
  • Sustainable livelihoods created

Environmental Sustainability Drive

This project focused on planting over 1500 trees, distributing 1100+ reusable cloth bags, and conducting awareness campaigns to promote anti-plastic usage and environmental protection.

Engaging communities through eco-friendly activities, the initiative aimed to foster sustainable living and reduce plastic pollution.

Project Highlights:

  • Planted 1500+ trees near River Cauvery
  • Distributed 1100+ reusable cloth bags
  • Anti-plastic usage awareness campaigns
  • Community engagement through sports & events
  • Collaboration with local schools and villages
  • Increased environmental consciousness
  • Promoted sustainable alternatives
  • Youth involvement through kabaddi tournaments

Sapphire Blue Social Trust is committed to empowering marginalized communities through education, health, and skill development programs across Tamil Nadu.
